About Me

London, Ontario, Canada

I'm a Data Analyst with an MBA in Software Engineering and a BS in Information Science, bringing over 15 years of experience in data analysis, ETL, and Business Intelligence.

My most significant experience was at the Health Department of the Federal District (Brazil), where I worked for over 9 years. There, I led the creation of the "Situation Room" portal — a Business Intelligence system that provides strategic health indicators for government managers and the general public, still in use today.

In Canada, I worked at Cognition+ for nearly 4 years, solving complex data issues in Oracle production environments for insurance clients. More recently, I served as a consultant at York University, conducting statistical analyses for a mental health study, including logistic regression and data preparation for academic publication.

This diverse background has given me a unique ability to adapt to different business contexts, consistently delivering results through clear and actionable insights.
